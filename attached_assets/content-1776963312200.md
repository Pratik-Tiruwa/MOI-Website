## Why Most Influencer Marketing Breaks at Scale

Most brands are forced to choose between two imperfect options. Neither works long-term.


### The Consultancy Problem

Great at spreadsheets, frameworks, and decks but poor at building content that platforms reward.
The thinking is sound, but the output lacks cultural relevance, speed, and algorithmic fit.

### The Creator-First Problem

Great at making visually appealing content but weak on unit economics, brand risk, and scalability.
The vibe works, but the business logic doesn’t.

Most brands don’t need to choose between logic and vibe.

They need both working together.
